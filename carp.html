Principles of Design

Contrast, Proximity, Repetition & Alignment:

Four of the core principles for good quality designs and layouts. I think when I came to doing the second drafts/iterations, an increase in the quality of my work can be seen for most of the posters. For this task I mostly just tried stuff until it seemed to click in my brain, but I found this video by Gareth David Studio useful in diving deeper into these.
